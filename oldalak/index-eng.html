Storyline

The Cuphead Show takes place in the 1930s style world of Inkwell Isles! follows the adventures of the impulsive, trouble-making old man Cuphead and the cautious but easily swayed Mugman, a pair of anthropomorphic cup brothers who live with their grandfather and elderly caretaker, Elder Kettle, in a teapot-shaped cottage. The brothers' stories often center around them trying to find their way out of various problems, occasionally meeting and interacting with characters from the video game source material. Although the series is mainly episodic, one recurring plot is the Devil hunting Cuphead for his soul, which he believes is rightfully his, after Cuphead loses a soul-collecting game called "Soul Ball", which is always thwarted by Cuphead and Mugman.

Trivia

  • The animation of Cuphead's head as a planet and Mugman flying around it in an airplane at the beginning of every episode is a homage to the old title card for Universal Studios.
  • The show's dialogue calls Hell "The Underworld" so it doesn't offend parents. The audio description, however, still calls it "Hell" in some episodes.

Crazy credits

  • The series opens with Mugman in a biplane circling a giant Cuphead's cup. This a parody of the 1930s Universal Pictures logo, which reflects the 1930s setting of the show.
